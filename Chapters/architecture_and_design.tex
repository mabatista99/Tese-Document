\chapter{Design and Architecture}
\label{cha:ArchitectureAndDesign}

This chapter defines the proposed platform from a design point of view. It starts by presenting the use cases in Section~\ref{sec:UseCases} and the requirements derived from them in Section~\ref{sec:RequirementsSpecification}. It then describes the overall architecture in Section~\ref{sec:ProposedModelArchitecturalOverview}, the edge node design in Section~\ref{sec:EdgeNode}, the main data flows in Section~\ref{sec:ProposedModelDataFlowAndInteraction}, and the data model and storage design in Section~\ref{sec:ProposedModelDataModelAndStorageDesign}. Together, these sections explain what the platform is expected to do and how its main components are organised to support those goals.
