\section{Use Cases}
\label{sec:UseCases}

This section presents the end-user use cases that define the platform scope. The platform has a single actor, the End User, who interacts with the web application to monitor devices, review alerts, and analyse telemetry data. These use cases form the basis for the requirements in Section~\ref{sec:RequirementsSpecification} and for the architectural decisions discussed in this chapter. Table~\ref{tab:uc-end-user} summarises these use cases.

\begin{table}[htbp]
    \centering
    \caption{End User use cases.}
    \label{tab:uc-end-user}
    \small
    \rowcolors{2}{gray!8}{}
    \begin{tabularx}{\textwidth}{l l X}
        \toprule
        \textbf{ID} & \textbf{Name}                            & \textbf{Description}                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  \\
        \midrule
        UC01        & Monitor Devices in Real Time             & Register devices and monitor their current state through live telemetry and real-time dashboards.                                                                                                                                         \\
        UC02        & Automate Anomaly Detection               & Configure monitoring rules and anomaly detection parameters so the platform can detect problems and send notifications without continuous user monitoring.                                                                                \\
        UC03        & Review and Acknowledge Alerts            & Review alerts triggered by rules or statistical anomalies, inspect the relevant context, and acknowledge alerts that have been handled.                                                                                                \\
        UC04        & Investigate Device History               & Query historical telemetry and past alerts for a device over a selected time range to analyse previous behaviour or incidents.                                                                                                           \\
        UC05        & Monitor Devices During Connectivity Loss & Monitor devices at sites with unreliable cloud connectivity, with local ingestion, rule evaluation, and alert generation continuing at the edge and buffered data synchronized with the cloud when the connection returns.             \\
        \bottomrule
    \end{tabularx}
\end{table}

Together, these use cases cover real-time monitoring, automatic detection, alert handling, historical analysis, and continued monitoring during connectivity loss. Supporting actions such as authentication, device registration, rule creation, and notification settings are treated as part of these user goals rather than as separate use cases.
